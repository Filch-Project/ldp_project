Thanks for checking out my website!

Hello, my name is Adam and I enjoy writing programs and building websites. I also enjoy playing fantasy football. The only problem is that I really don't follow football closely at all. So when it comes to draft time I am generally as blind as a bat. I usually print off the top 100 players and draft whomever the most highly ranked player is for whatever position I am choosing to fill. I felt there had to be a better way to decide which position would be the next logical choice.

Since I am a data analyst by profession I decided to write a program to assit me with just that. This website is the result of what I have accomplished so far working in my spare time. Unfortunatly, the process is slow going since I don't have a lot of spare time and I am still learning how to do all of this from scratch.

While I am aware that the website is not fancy and doesn't have a lot of bells and whistles, it is functional and gets the job done. I am choosing to roll it out in the beginning stages to gain user feedback as to the type of enhancements that would help improve it's functionality.

I plan to send out a survey later on in the season. So be on the look out for that. Any feedback or suggestions you have to offer will be greatly appreciated and I will try to implement as many of them as I can for the next season.

I hope that this website makes/made your draft a whole lot easier.

Thanks,

Adam
